About us

Christ Image Assembly is not just a place of worship, It is a place where you can connect with God, get to know him more and experience transformational encounters.
We are empowering people to use what God has given them to serve and love our world through relational disciple-making. Man is God's ambassador on earth to take control over all other creatures on His behalf on earth-Gen. 1:26-28.
We seek to reach out to those who are not currently active in their relationship with Jesus Christ. It is our goal to see people do more than simply attend church, but also grow in their faith.

Vision & Mission statement

  • To make Heaven.
  • To take as many people with us.
  • To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations.
  • To accomplish No. 1 above, Holiness would be our lifestyle.
  • To accomplish No. 2 and 3 above, we will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries and within five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries.
  • We will pursue this objective until every Nation is reached for the Lord Jesus Christ
